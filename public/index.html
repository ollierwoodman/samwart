Samuel Woodman Art

My work takes the form of painted abstract diagrams, delineating the systems by which we explain and become aware of the world around us. I paint in an attempt to understand my own introspection; to try to fathom the knowledge systems of the world and of the self. I have a hunger to make sense of the methods by which meaning is created. I do this by creating and visualising my own system, taking the scientific, religious and philosophical and turning it into symbol, colour and form. Through this, I strive to question what the world means, what I mean, what anything means. While I believe that this task is impossible, I find beauty, meaning and purpose in trying to carry it out.
